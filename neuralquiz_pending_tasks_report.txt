Daily Challenge module
Goal

Create a module that automatically generates or assigns new challenges every day based on the user’s target company, career roadmap stage, and previous progress.

Requirements

Generate fresh daily challenges automatically at midnight in the user’s local time.

Personalize challenges using the target company selected in Career Roadmap, the current roadmap stage, the user’s performance and weak areas, and existing modules such as Interview, Public Speaking, and NeuralQuiz.

Support challenge categories including Coding/DSA, Technical Interview Questions, HR Interview Questions, Public Speaking Tasks, Quizzes, System Design Tasks, and Company-Specific Preparation Tasks.

Include difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.

Allow users to mark challenges as completed, skip challenges, and add notes or answers.

Track and store daily streak count, total challenges completed, weekly and monthly completion rates, points or badge rewards, and challenge history.

Daily Challenge UI

Display today’s challenge in a highlighted card.

Show category, difficulty, estimated time, and completion status.

Include Complete, Skip, and Add Notes actions.

Display streak count and past challenge history.
